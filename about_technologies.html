Technologies

BECOME AN ELITE GOLFER
WITH TECHNOLOGY USED BY THE PROS

Take your game to a whole new level with access to Leadbetter Golf Academy’s 11 high-tech hitting bays, equipped with GC2 launch monitors and Swing Catalyst software. Our advanced technology gives you hard data that pinpoints where your game needs improvement and provides you multi-angle video replay of every swing.

The Trackman, Boditrak, Sam Putt Lab and other high-tech equipment available to you at Leadbetter Golf Academy is rarely accessible to the average golfer. Not only will it help you learn faster, but you’ll also experience how tour professionals train as you gain long-term game improvement!

TECHNOLOGY WE OFFER

FORESIGHT

Improve your game with the most realistic ball flight simulation ever. For every shot you hit, data from our Foresight GC2 launch monitor will reveal your clubhead speed, launch angle, carry & offline distance, spin rates, and much more.

FSX SOFTWARE

Experience the best golf simulation imaginable. From the driving range to the simulated golf courses, the accuracy and intelligence of the FSX software gives you an extremely realistic feel that’s the closest thing to being on an actual golf course.

Every course detail is recreated with exacting precision, and when ready you’ll have access to a range of world class courses at your fingertips. Play 18 holes in any format you wish, while in the comfort of the Leadbetter Golf Academy’s luxury hitting bays.

ZOMBIE GOLF

Grab you club, ball and tee, and get ready to vanquish hordes of ZOMBIES! As you fight off the undead mob in this battle against the clock, you’ll fine-tune your shot’s accuracy while having fun! Players can choose to either take out approaching zombies, or focus their shots on the crypts where the undead are spawned. If you’re looking for the ultimate skill-building game to improve your shot, Zombie Golf is the perfect choice for all ages.

TRACK MAN

Fine-tune your game using the same technology as over 150 current PGA tour players. Trusted by some of the best golfers to ever play the game, Trackman provides you a complete analysis of your swing that enables you to drive harder, further and with more precision. For countless teaching professionals and golfers of all skill levels, Trackman is a game-changer. But don’t just take our word for it. Listen to what the best in the golf industry have to say about Trackman.
