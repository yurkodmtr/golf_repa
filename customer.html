SOME OF THE MORE POPULAR MINI TOPICS INCLUDE:

Faults & Fixes – add PGA polish to your game and improve your fundamentals as you fix inconsistent strikes, club faces, slices, hooks and other common faults.

The A Swing – learn the swing technique David Leadbetter taught PGA professionals as he coached them to 22 Major Championship titles.

Handicap Reduction – if you feel embarrassed by your handicap, this mini lesson maps out a plan that’s guaranteed to reduce yours.

Increasing Driver Distance – drive harder and farther. With this mini lesson, you’ll gain easier shots to the greens and bragging rights among your friends.

Short Game Secrets – learn how to save the most shots of your game by improving where it counts—from 100 yards and in. Practice with a Purpose – no longer waste time hitting balls and hoping for improvement. This package teaches you the practice routines of PGA pros.

On Course Instruction – get hands on training as you play 18 holes with a PGA instructor. You’ll learn course management, pre shot routines and much more.

Tournament Preparation – prepare to win as our PGA instructors teach you the same techniques that helped them achieve victory at prestigious tournaments.

Golf Performance (Fitness) – reduce injury while you increase strength, speed and flexibility that will optimize your body and swing for maximum performance.

Whether you just wish to focus on one of these mini lessons or a mixture of several, we can design a lesson package that will improve your game and give you more confidence on the green.
